Guided Study Advice

1) Make a plan you can actually finish

Choose one main outcome for the session. Split it into 25–40 minute blocks. Keep a buffer of 20% time for surprises.

2) Sequence matters

Do hardest topic first two blocks when willpower is highest. Practice questions before re-reading notes.

3) Active over passive

Write, solve, teach out loud, use flashcards. Reading alone is slow learning.

4) Review loop

End each session with a 3-minute summary and list blockers for next time.

5) Protect your focus

Silence notifications. Tell people you’re in a session. Keep water/snack ready so you don’t roam.
